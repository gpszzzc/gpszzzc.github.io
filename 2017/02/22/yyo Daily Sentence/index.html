02/22

Over the past couple of decades, however, downtown Los Angeles has undergone^1 a major revival, thanks in part to[^2] the introduction[^3] of the Adaptive Reuse Ordinance[^4] in 1999.

[^2]: thanks to:由于，归功于
due to: 用于较庄重的书面语中，侧重“起因于”，在句中多作表语，有时作状语。
owing to:可以和due to换用，但在句中多作状语，也可作表语。
because of:着重某种原因的理由，在句中通常作状语。
thanks to:突出一种感激之情，含“多亏”意味。

[^3]: introduction:n. 介绍, 引见,引言, 导论,引进, 传入; 采用,新采用的东西

[^4]: Ordinance: /‘ɔrdnəns/ n. <正>条例,法令

02/23

The Otto technology enables complete autonomy on highways: trucks can navigate, stay in their lane, and slow or stop in response to[^5] traffic conditions completely without human intervention[^6].

[^5]: in response to:响应，对……有反应
[^6]: intervention: /ˌɪntɚ’vɛnʃən/ 介入，干涉，干预

They were rather handsome, had been educated in one of the first private seminaries in town, had a fortune of twenty thousand pounds, were in the habit of spending more than they ought, and of associating with people of rank; and were therefore in every respect entitled to think well of themselves, and meanly of others.
